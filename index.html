About My Village

Bình Định was probably one of the places where the Cham first arrived in what is now Vietnam. Its favourable geography led to the rise of the Cham Port of Thị Nại where Quy Nhơn is now located.

The city-state of Vijaya was located around this port and the main city, which was further inland. Its centre was in the southern lowland of Bình Định.
